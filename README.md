# price-calculator

This is a simple web application designed to help users calculate the price per pound or kilogram of a large bag and then determine the cost of smaller bags based on the calculated price per unit. It's especially useful for retail environments where items are sold by weight and need to be priced individually in a POS system.

## Features

- **Price Per Unit Calculation:** Input the weight and price of a large bag to calculate the price per pound or kilogram.
- **Cost Per Small Bag Calculation:** Input the weight of smaller bags to calculate their cost based on the price per unit of the large bag.
- **POS System Instructions:** Step-by-step guide on how to apply the calculated prices in a POS system.

## How to Use

1. **Calculate Price Per Unit:**
   - Enter the weight (in pounds or kilograms) of the large bag in the "Big Bag Weight" field.
   - Enter the total price of the large bag in the "Big Bag Price" field.
   - Click the "Calculate Price Per Unit" button to get the price per pound or kilogram.

2. **Calculate Cost Per Small Bag:**
   - After calculating the price per unit, enter the weight of the smaller bag in the "Small Bag Weight" field.
   - Click the "Calculate Cost Per Bag" button to get the cost of the small bag.

3. **Apply in POS System:**
   - Use the calculated cost per small bag in your POS system by selecting "Change Price" after scanning the small bag.
